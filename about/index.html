About me

I'm a freelance Frontend Developer

Angular & TypeScript are my daily drivers, but I like to dive into other topics as well.

Over the years I've built up a love for creating reactive, well-designed web applications. Test driven development is a practice I like to use and over the years I have grown to love more and more.

I'm an active person within the Angular community and on Twitter. Since 2019 I'm part of the Core Team of NG-BE, a yearly Angular conference in Ghent.

Although my main focus is the frontend space, you'll also can find me exploring the likes Rust, Deno or Flutter.

I'm 38 years old, living in Belgium with my wife and 2 amazing daughters. I enjoy walks, watching a good series or movie, building LEGO sets and spending time with family & friends.
